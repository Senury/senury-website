## Dependencies & Risks

### Critical Dependencies

1. **Technical Resources**
   - Risk: Developer availability
   - Mitigation: Hire freelancer if needed
   - Impact: Delays all technical tasks

2. **Content Quality**
   - Risk: Finding German legal tech writer
   - Mitigation: Start with founder content
   - Impact: Delays content calendar

3. **Regulatory Changes**
   - Risk: Changes to §203 StGB or DSGVO
   - Mitigation: Monitor BNotK announcements
   - Impact: Requires content updates

### Risk Matrix

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Developer unavailability | Medium | High | Freelancer backup |
| Content delays | Medium | Medium | Founder involvement |
| Algorithm updates | Medium | Medium | Focus on quality |
| Competitor response | Low | Medium | Differentiation |
| Budget constraints | Low | High | Prioritize Phase 1 |
